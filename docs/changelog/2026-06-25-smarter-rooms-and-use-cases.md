**Version:** v0.2
**Date:** 2026-06-25

## Smarter AI Debate Rooms and New Use Cases ✨

MadClub now feels more like a real multi-agent debate room: you can speak to AI advisors, follow clearer live signals, explore practical use cases, and track your session minutes.

This release also adds the first foundation for referrals. You can find your referral link in [Usage & Referrals](/account/usage).

## 🚀 What's New

- **Interactive AI debates** – Hosts can now use their microphone to guide, interrupt, and shape the conversation while agents keep the room moving.
- **Clearer live sessions** – Speaking, thinking, and "you're live" indicators make it easier to understand what each participant is doing.
- **Better session history** – Recordings, conversation details, and safer history removal are now part of the account experience.
- **New homepage use cases** – Startup validation, customer discovery, decision councils, learning rooms, pitch roasts, interviews, workplace coaching, and negotiation practice now have distinct room visuals.
- **Usage and referrals** – Session-minute balances, referral links, first-session referral rewards, and start-session checks are now being wired into the product.

## 🛠️ Under the Hood

- **Production-ready infrastructure** – `madclub.ai`, Cognito, LiveKit, backend agents, media storage, and CORS are now configured for the real environment.
- **More reliable debate runtime** – Agent turns, host interruption, recordings, room cleanup, and session lifecycle handling are now more robust.
- **Generated room artwork** – Scenario and use-case images now use the backend's generated-cover style system for more distinct AI advisor rooms.

## 🕹️ What to Try Next

1. **Browse the new use cases** and pick a room for your next idea.
2. **Start an AI debate** and steer it with your own prompt or voice.
3. **Share your referral link** from [Usage & Referrals](/account/usage).
