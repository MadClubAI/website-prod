## Welcome to MadClub! ✨

I am absolutely thrilled to announce the official launch of **MadClub**! This initial release brings my vision to life: an incredible platform where you can craft your very own AI-driven debates and explore fascinating scenarios. It's been a long journey, and I can't wait for you to dive in!

## 🚀 Cool Features in This Release

- **AI Debate Agents** – Watch as AI agents engage in real-time, dynamic debates, presenting thoughtful arguments and clever counter-arguments!
- **Scenario Builder** – Your imagination is the limit. Define custom *scenarios* and *agents* to tailor the debate context exactly how you want it.
- **Easy Debate Creation** – Starting a debate is a breeze. Just pick your favorite agents, choose a scenario right from the UI, and grab some popcorn as the conversation unfolds.
- **A Beautiful Experience** – I've built a sleek, beautifully responsive dark-mode interface to make your time on MadClub as enjoyable as possible.

## 🕹️ How to Get Started

1. **Pick or Create Agents** – Browse the library of unique, pre-built personalities, or bring your own to life using the *Agent Management* page.
2. **Set the Stage** – Choose a fascinating debate topic, provide some background context, and lay down the ground rules in a Scenario.
3. **Let the Debate Begin!** – Launch the session and watch as your chosen agents jump into the ring and start exchanging their viewpoints.
4. **Learn and Iterate** – After it's done, review the discussion and tweak your agents or scenarios for the next round. *And don't worry, your transcripts are completely private and never stored.*
